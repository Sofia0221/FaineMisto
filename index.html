About the event

Festival movement

Ukrainian open-air music festival, held annually in Ternopil since 2013. In 2013, the festival was held on the island "Seagull" in the park named after Taras Shevchenko. Its founders are Maxim Cherkashin, Rostislav Plakhtsinsky, Vladimir Pasyaka and Rustam Ergeshov. Since 2014, "Fine City" has moved to the territory of Ternopil Airport. In 2017, the festival moved to the Ternopil racetrack. The location is better because there are trees and a lake. The best festival of 2015 according to the magazine "Step into the Rock".

Changes

In 2022, the festival expects changes related to paid camping, which has the Hollywood name "Beverly Hills". It is planned to better organize the work of the paid site. The territory of the tent camp is planned to be divided into streets, alleys, neighborhoods. And so now there will be signs and street names. So the quest to find the streets will be much easier. Street names are currently being selected. They can be offered on the festival's Facebook page.
